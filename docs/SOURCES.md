- Park C, Ferrell AJ, Meade N, Shen PS, Walsh D, et al. Distinct non-canonical translation initiation modes arise for specific host and viral mRNAs during poxvirus-induced shutoff. Nature Microbiology. 2025;10:1535-1549. doi:10.1038/s41564-025-02009-4.
